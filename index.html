Our Vision

  • To create a sustainable future by integrating environmental, social, and economic sustainability into education systems.
  • Focus on achieving Sustainable Development Goals 4 (Quality Education), 13 (Climate Action), and 17 (Partnerships for the Goals).
  • Envision education systems in Malaysia that prioritize sustainability, environmental responsibility, and collaborative innovation to build a better future for all.

Our Mission

  • To empower educators, students, policymakers, and community members through collaboration, innovative resources, and impactful initiatives that promote sustainable development.
  • Strive to provide educators with the tools and strategies they need to teach sustainability effectively, engage students in hands-on learning experiences, and foster community partnerships that support sustainable practices.
  • Our long-term goal is to create a more sustainable and equitable society for future generations.

About the ESD Alliance

The ESD Alliance is a collaborative network of educators and organizations working to advance sustainable education aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

UNESCO Partnership Goal

Alliance ESD Malaysia aims to become a member of UNESCO's Greening Education Partnership, a global initiative transforming education systems to tackle climate change and support sustainable development.
